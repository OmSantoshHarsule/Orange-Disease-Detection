Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions farmers and users ask about our orange disease detection platform. We aim to make crop health management simple and accessible for everyone.

If you have any other queries, feel free to reach out through our contact page or support email.

How does the orange disease detection system work?
Our system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model trained on thousands of orange leaf images. Just upload a photo of the leaf, and the system will analyze and predict the type of disease in seconds.
What kind of images should I upload?
You should upload a clear image of the orange leaf, preferably taken in natural light. Ensure that the diseased area is visible and not blurry or shadowed for accurate results.
Which orange diseases can this detect?
Currently, our model can detect the following diseases: Citrus Canker, Greasy Spot, Citrus Scab, and Black Spot. More diseases will be added in future updates as we expand our dataset.
Is this service free to use?
Yes, our platform is currently free for all users. We aim to support farmers and agricultural researchers by providing accessible plant health diagnostics.
